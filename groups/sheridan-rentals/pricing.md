# Sheridan Trailer Rentals — Pricing

## RV Camper
- **Rate**: $150/night
- **Deposit**: $250 (refundable)
- Deposit refunded when returned in good condition

## Car Hauler
- **Rate**: $65/day
- **Deposit**: $50 (refundable)
- **Trailer weight**: 1,800 lbs
- **Capacity**: ~6,000 lbs
- Includes: straps, ramps, winch, spare tire
- Deposit refunded when returned in good condition

## Landscaping Trailer
- **Rate**: $50/day
- **Deposit**: $50 (refundable)
- Includes: dolly for furniture/appliances
- Deposit refunded when returned in good condition

## Add-Ons (RV Only)

### Generator
- **Rate**: $75/night
- Includes 5 gallons of gas

### Delivery
- **Rate**: $250 flat
- Covers pickup + dropoff within 60 miles of Tomball, TX

## After-Hours Pickup / Drop-Off

- **Standard hours**: 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM, 7 days a week (matches GBP)
- **After-hours fee**: **$25 per occurrence** (each pickup OR drop-off outside 8 AM – 8 PM)
- Applies to all equipment; Car Hauler is the most common request and we accommodate 24/7
- Quote it when customer asks for late-night/early-morning pickup or drop-off
- Add the fee as a line item in the booking notes and collect on pickup or via Square invoice

## Payment Terms

### RV Camper Payment Policy
- **Same-week booking** (rental starts within 7 days): Full payment required upfront (rental + $250 deposit)
- **Advance booking** (rental starts >7 days out): Customer can choose:
  - **Option A — Deposit only**: Pay $250 deposit to reserve the dates. Remaining rental balance due before pickup.
  - **Option B — Pay in full**: Pay everything upfront (rental + deposit). This is always available.
- The booking form automatically shows these options based on the selected dates

### Car Hauler & Landscaping Trailer
- Full payment required at booking (rental + deposit)

### General
- All payments via Square online checkout
- Payment confirmation unlocks lock code for trailer access
- Refundable security deposits ($250 RV, $50 haulers/trailers) refunded upon return in good condition
- Standard pickup/drop-off hours: 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM, 7 days a week. After-hours = $25 per occurrence (see above)
