# Sheridan Trailer Rentals FAQs

Approved answers for common questions.

## What do you rent out?
We have three rentals: an RV Camper ($150/night), a Car Hauler ($65/day), and a Landscaping Trailer ($50/day). Check pricing.md for full details.

## Do I need a special license?
No, a regular driver's license works. Just need a vehicle with the right hitch and towing capacity.

## What's included with the Car Hauler?
Straps, ramps, winch, and a spare tire — everything you need to haul a vehicle.

## How much does the Car Hauler weigh / what's the capacity?
The trailer itself weighs 1,800 lbs with a capacity of about 6,000 lbs.

## What's included with the Landscaping Trailer?
Comes with a dolly for moving furniture and appliances.

## Can I add a generator to the RV?
Yes, generator rental is $75/night and includes 5 gallons of gas.

## Do you deliver?
Yes, delivery is $250 flat for the RV Camper — covers pickup and dropoff within 60 miles of Tomball. Car Hauler and Landscaping Trailer are pickup only.

## Is there a deposit?
Yes, refundable security deposit required at booking:
- RV Camper: $250
- Car Hauler: $50
- Landscaping Trailer: $50
You get it back when equipment comes back in good shape.

## How does payment work?
For the **RV Camper**: If your rental starts within the same week, full payment (rental + $250 deposit) is required at booking. If you're booking more than a week out, you can either pay the $250 deposit to reserve your dates (remaining balance due before pickup) or pay everything upfront — your choice. The booking form shows both options automatically.

For **Car Hauler** and **Landscaping Trailer**: Full payment + deposit is required at booking.

Once full payment is received, you get the lock code to access the equipment. Book at sheridantrailerrentals.us/form/

## What are your hours?
We're always open — no restricted hours. Pick up and drop off at your convenience.

## What if I need to extend my rental?
Just text or call us. We'll check availability and extend your booking if the equipment is open.

## When do I get the lock code?
The lock code is sent via text/email as soon as full payment clears — usually within minutes of checkout. If you paid a deposit only, you'll get the lock code once the remaining balance is paid before pickup. If you don't receive it within 15 minutes of paying, text us and we'll send it right away.

## How does the remaining balance work for deposit-only bookings?
If you booked the RV with deposit only, we'll reach out before your pickup date with a payment link for the remaining balance. Payment must be completed before your rental starts. Once the balance is paid, you'll get the lock code.

## When do I get my deposit refund back?
Your refund is processed within 3-5 business days after returning the equipment in good condition. It goes back to the same card you paid with. Normal wear and tear (dirt, dust, minor scratches) doesn't affect your refund — we only deduct for actual damage that costs money to fix.

## Can I modify my booking (add generator, change dates)?
Text or call us with your change. We'll check availability and send you an updated quote. We handle modifications on our end — no need to rebook through the form.

## Can I pay with Venmo, cash, or something other than a card?
Our standard checkout is through Square (credit/debit card). If you need an alternative, just ask — we can sometimes work something out.

## What's your cancellation policy?
Contact us as early as possible. Cancellations more than 7 days before the rental start date get a full refund. Less than 7 days, there may be a cancellation fee. See terms.md for details.
