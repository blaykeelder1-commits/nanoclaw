# Sheridan Trailer Rentals Sales Playbook

## Upsell Techniques

### RV Customers
- Offer the generator ($75/night) — "Want a generator with that? Includes 5 gallons of gas so you're set."
- Offer delivery ($250) — "We can deliver and pick up within 60 miles of Tomball. Saves you the hassle."
- Suggest longer stays for better value

### Car Hauler Customers
- Mention everything's included (straps, ramps, winch, spare tire) — they get a lot for $65/day
- If they need it for multiple days, emphasize the low daily rate

### Landscaping Trailer Customers
- Mention the dolly is included for furniture/appliances
- If they're moving, suggest the Car Hauler if they also need to move a vehicle

## Common Objections

### "That's more than I expected"
- "Totally understand. Keep in mind everything's included — no hidden fees. And deposits are fully refundable."

### "I'm not sure my truck can tow that"
- "No worries, what do you drive? I can check if it'll work and suggest the right option."

### "I'll think about it"
- "No rush. Just keep in mind weekends book up fast. Want me to hold a date for you?"

### "Can I get a discount?"
- "Our rates are already pretty competitive, but let me check with the team."

### "$250 deposit is a lot"
- "Totally get it. The deposit is fully refundable — you get every penny back when the equipment comes back in good shape. Normal wear and tear doesn't count against you. And if you're booking more than a week out, you can just pay the $250 deposit now and the rest before pickup."

### "The booking form is confusing" / "This is too complicated"
- "Hey, I can make this simpler. All you do is tap the link, pick your dates, fill in your info, and pay — takes about 2 minutes. Want me to walk you through it?"
- If still hesitant: "Or just tell me your dates and I'll check availability right now. If it works, I'll send you the link and you're 2 clicks away."

### Customer seems about to abandon
Watch for: "never mind", "I'll think about it", "forget it", "too expensive", "I'll find something else"
- Acknowledge: "No pressure at all. Just so you know, weekends book up fast."
- Offer flexibility: "If the timing or price isn't right, let me see what I can do."
- Last resort: "Let me check with my boss — we might be able to work something out." → Email owner with: "Customer hesitating on [equipment] for [dates]. Possible save opportunity. They said: [reason]."

## Closing Strategies

- Mention weekends and holidays book fast
- Make deposit payment easy — send them the link
- Emphasize the simple process: "Book online, pay upfront or put a deposit down, and you're all set"
- Remaining balance due before pickup — no surprises at the door
- Deposits are refundable — low risk for the customer
