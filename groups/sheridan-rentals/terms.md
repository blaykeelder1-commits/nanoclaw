# Sheridan Trailer Rentals Terms

## Rental Agreement
- Valid driver's license required
- Must have appropriate tow vehicle
- Renter is responsible for the equipment during rental period

## Deposits
- RV Camper: $250 refundable deposit
- Car Hauler: $50 refundable deposit
- Landscaping Trailer: $50 refundable deposit
- Paid at booking via Square online payment
- Refunded within 3-5 business days of return in good condition (back to same card)
- Normal wear and tear (dirt, dust, minor exterior scratches) does NOT affect refund
- Only actual damage that costs money to repair is deducted

## Payment

### RV Camper
- **Same-week booking** (starts within 7 days): Full payment + deposit required upfront
- **Advance booking** (starts >7 days out): Customer chooses deposit-only or pay-in-full at checkout
  - Deposit-only: $250 deposit reserves the dates, remaining balance due before pickup
  - Pay-in-full: Everything charged at booking
- Lock code sent via text/email within minutes of full payment clearing

### Balance Collection (Deposit-Only RV Bookings)
- We send a payment link before the pickup date for the remaining balance
- Balance must be paid before rental starts
- Lock code released only after full payment received
- If balance is not paid by pickup time, rental may be cancelled

### Car Hauler & Landscaping Trailer
- Full payment + deposit required at booking

### General
- Book and pay at sheridantrailerrentals.us/form/
- We're always open — no restricted pickup/dropoff hours

## Cancellation Policy
- Contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel
- Cancellations require owner approval — Andy will check and get back to you
- More than 7 days before rental: full refund
- Less than 7 days: may forfeit a portion of the deposit
- Less than 24 hours: deposit may not be refundable
- Andy will confirm the decision and refund timeline within a few hours

## Late Returns
- Contact us if you need to extend — we'll check availability
- Unauthorized late returns may result in additional charges

## Damage
- Renter responsible for damage during rental period
- Normal wear and tear excluded
- Report any issues immediately
